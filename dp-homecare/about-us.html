My aim is to provide access to doctors from different specialities who can be consulted by anyone from anywhere in the world. This can vary from consulting a doctor online to knowing an alternative treatment for any ailment or in exploring other low-cost treatments available. A patient with any medical condition will have the option to choose a doctor or multiple doctors to get a medical opinion or advise.

DP Second Opinion allows you to make your own panel of super specialists, specialists or any great doctor offering their service online. You can write your medical condition and upload your reports on our platform and choose a doctor or doctors as you wish.

This information will then be shared with doctor/s you selected who will review your case independently and give their opinion. There is also an option for the patient can to speak with their chosen doctor/s.

Patient has access to DP Second Opinion service across the day as its available online so that any patient can reach any doctors at any time, without leaving home.
